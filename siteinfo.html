What is this site?

This is a website for me to share my music hobby!
Each year I have the goal of listening to at least 100 new albums. At the end of the year I make lists of my favorite albums and songs I listened to, and this website is the place for me to share those lists with others!
On the main page select the year you want, and then choose between the two lists:
  • Favorite Albums
  • Favorite Songs
This site is centered around albums, so that is the list I focus on more. The "Favorite Songs" list is to let me recommend songs I like, even if I didn't like the album it was from.
If you are looking for certain genres of music, prefer listening to whole albums, or want to browse album art, I would suggest using the albums list. Otherwise, you can use the "Favorite Songs" to get a quicker sample of each year's music.

Rating system

There are two components to how I organize/rate the music on my lists:
First, each list is in order from favorite to least favorite, with the first album/song being my favorite.
Second, I organize albums into different "tiers." I think there is a noteworthy difference between the albums in different tiers. I use colors to indicate these tiers, which is why you will see some albums colored as gold, silver, and bronze.
The tiers roughly correlate to:
  • Gold = Best albums of the year.
  • Silver = Great albums that are strong highlights of the year.
  • Bronze = Good albums that are notable releases of the year.
  • Uncolored = Honorable mentions that I wanted to include.
One thing to note is that I listen to a lot more albums than are shown on these lists. So, every album listed here is something I actually really like, even if it appears "low" on my list.

Development & updates

This is purely a personal passion project, so things won't be too fancy and updates will be infrequent. With that said, feedback/suggestions are always accepted and appreciated if there is anything you would like to see added or changed!
I plan on updating this with new music every few months or so. This is primarily meant to share my year-end lists, rather than be constantly updated every time I listen to something new. So, there will be more updates towards the end of the year as I am making my big final year-end lists.
This website is hosted for free using GitHub Pages (which I highly recommend if you want to develop your own website froms scratch). You can see all the code for this website on its github repo at github.com/albumsfromkyle/albumsfromkyle.github.io/

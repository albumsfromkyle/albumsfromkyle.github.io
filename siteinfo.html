What is this site?

This is a website for me to share my music hobby!
Each year I have the goal of listening to at least 100 new albums. At the end of the year I make lists of my favorite albums and songs I listened to, and this website is the place for me to share those lists with others!
On the main page select the year you want, and then choose the list you want to see:
  • Favorite Albums
  • Favorite Songs
The "Favorite Songs" list lets me recommend songs I liked, even if I didn't like the albums it was from. So, it will have more variety on it. But there is a lot of overlap between these two lists, because my favorite albums normally have my favorite songs on them.
So if you are looking for certain genre(s) of music, or just prefer albums, I would suggest using the albums list. Otherwise, I would suggest listening to the "Favorite Songs" playlist to get a good taste of the music I recommend.

Rating system

There are two components to how I organize/rate the music on my lists:
First, each list is in order from favorite to least favorite, with the first album/song being my favorite.
Second, I use an olympic-like rating system to indicate different "tiers" of albums, meaning I think there is a noteworthy difference in gold albums vs. silver vs. bronze. This is why you will see certain albums highlighted in gold, silver, and bronze. As you would expect, gold is the best, then silver, then bronze.
In general, the tiers roughly correlate to:
  • Gold = Extremely good albums that are the best of the year.
  • Silver = Great albums that are strong highlights of the year.
  • Bronze = Very good albums that are notable releases of the year.
  • Uncolored = Good albums I still wanted to include, even if they didn't receive a coloring.
One thing to note is that I listen to a lot more albums than are shown on the lists. However, I only include the albums I really enjoyed and would actually recommend to people.

Development & updates

This website is hosted for free using GitHub Pages. You can see all the code for this website on its github repo at github.com/albumsfromkyle/albumsfromkyle.github.io/.
This is purely a personal passion project, so it definitely won't be fancy and updates will be infrequent (besides adding new music). With that said, feedback/suggestions are always accepted and appreciated if there is anything you would like to see added or changed!
I plan on updating this with new music every few months or so. This is primarily meant to share my year-end lists, rather than be constantly updated every time I listen to something new. So there will be more updates towards the end of the year, as I am making my big final changes to the lists.

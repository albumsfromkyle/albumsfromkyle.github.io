Discovering music

There are two main sites I use to discover music:
  • https://www.albumoftheyear.org/
  • https://rateyourmusic.com/
Both of those websites have lists for the user-voted best albums of the year, pages that outline recent releases, and a lot more. They are both great for getting a quick glance at new and popular music, and offer plenty of tools for discovering new and more niche music if that is what you want.

Other links

  • https://www.last.fm/ - Great for tracking your Spotify stats and listening habits throughout the year if you don't like only waiting for Spotify wrapped
  • https://exportify.net/ - Allows you to export spotify playlists as CSV files. This is a tool that I used when devlopming this website, and to back-up my own personal playlists.
  • https://www.music-map.com/ - Lets your explore other artists/bands similar to whatever artist/band you type in
